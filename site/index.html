Chrome Extension

Draw together, send playful effects, stay in control.

Sync Sketch Party is a social drawing extension that lets friends exchange live sketches and opt-in visual effects with clear privacy controls.

Privacy Policy Terms of Use

Store-friendly controls

Users can disable the extension, appear offline, or turn surprise effects off at any time from the popup.

Friend request approval

No one is added automatically. Every new connection must be accepted by the recipient.

Live social canvas

Friends can choose one-way send mode or synchronized drawing mode with live chat.
