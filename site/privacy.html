Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

What we collect

We store the profile name you enter, a generated user identifier, a generated device key, friend request state, accepted friend connections, and live drawing/chat session events required for the extension to function.

How we use data

Data is used only to provide identity, friend requests, online presence, drawing sessions, chat delivery, and user safety controls such as disable mode and surprise effect preferences.

What we do not collect

We do not collect browsing history for advertising, we do not sell personal data, and we do not intentionally inspect page content beyond rendering user-authorized visual overlays.

User controls

Users can disable the extension, appear offline, or disable surprise effects from the extension popup. When disabled, the extension should not allow incoming interactive sessions.

Data retention

For this MVP, operational data may be stored on the application backend. Data may be removed if infrastructure is reset, redeployed, or cleaned during maintenance.
